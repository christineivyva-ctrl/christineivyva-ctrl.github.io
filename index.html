My Process

How I work

01
Discovery

We start with a focused call to understand your business, your pain points, and what "good" looks like for you. No templates — just listening.

02
Audit

I review your current tools, workflows, and systems to identify gaps, bottlenecks, and quick wins — before touching anything.

03
Setup

I build or rebuild the systems we've agreed on — CRM, SOPs, dashboards, or operational workflows — with clear documentation from the start.

04
Optimize

Once systems are live, I monitor, refine, and improve — fixing friction points and making sure everything runs efficiently under real conditions.

05
Maintain

I stay on as your ongoing operational partner — keeping systems updated, handling daily execution, and growing with your business long-term.

How I Think

Operating principles

🗂
I document as I go

If it's not written down, it doesn't scale. SOPs get built alongside the work itself, not after the fact.

🎯
I own the problem, not just the task

I flag risk before it becomes urgent, and follow through until it's actually resolved.

🔄
I improve what I touch

Every recurring process is a candidate for a better version — I look for the friction and remove it.

🛡
I protect focus time

Calendars, inboxes, and priorities get guarded like they're my own — so decisions get made with a clear head.

📣
I communicate before I'm asked

Status updates happen proactively. Nobody should have to chase me for where something stands.

🧩
I build for handoff

Systems should outlast the person who built them — documented clearly enough that anyone can pick them up.
