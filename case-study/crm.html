Custom Sales CRM for a High-Ticket Business Coach

Overview

Built a custom Google Sheets CRM system to help a business coach organize leads, track follow-ups, and monitor sales progress in one centralized workspace.

The Problem

The client was handling leads manually across multiple spreadsheets and messages. Follow-ups were inconsistent, and there was no clear way to track sales progress.

My Role

I designed the CRM structure, organized lead stages, built the sales tracker, and created a reporting dashboard for visibility.

Tools Used

Google Sheets • Gmail • Google Drive • ChatGPT

Results

✔ Improved lead visibility
✔ Faster follow-up process
✔ Reduced manual tracking
✔ Better client organization
